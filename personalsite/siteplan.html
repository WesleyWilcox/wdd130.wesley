Overview

Purpose

I am making a portfolio website to display my artworks in a digitized and organized manner.

Who is this for?

Anyone that is interested in my work, whether it be potential investors or individuals requesting assistance or inspiration for their own works. I hope that what you find here will help you.

Why?

Uploading my art in a neat, ordered list will be helpful in letting anyone including me access my art history.
